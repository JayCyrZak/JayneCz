# Melion

## Gameplay
- Online multiplayer
- Top-down 2D
- Movement on Google Map
- Moving paints the map's surface
- Competitive mode: 2 teams, 5 minutes, the team that paints the most wins
- Free mode: Creative painting, solo, with friends, or globally
